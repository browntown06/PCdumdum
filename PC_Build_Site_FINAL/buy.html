Where to Buy PC Parts

Here are some trustworthy sources for buying PC parts. These stores are known for reliability, selection, and support:

  • Newegg - Great for deals and a wide selection of parts.
  • Amazon - Huge inventory, fast shipping, but be cautious of third-party sellers.
  • Micro Center - Physical stores with knowledgeable staff and frequent discounts.
  • B&H Photo Video - Trusted retailer for tech and electronics.

Tips:

  1. Check for refurbished or open-box items with a warranty.
  2. Compare prices using PCPartPicker.
  3. Always read reviews, especially for third-party sellers.
